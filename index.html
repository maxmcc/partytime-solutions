Gallery Opening Event

The Steiner & Co. art gallery, which is definitely a real thing that exists, will host an opening party on Saturday the 16th of April 2022 at 6pm. The gallery will showcase custom artwork from a range of talented artists attending the event (including you). Guests will participate in a silent auction and will bid for the opportunity to bring home one of the items on display, with all auction proceeds donated to charity.

Register your attendance →

Make and Bring Your Own Artwork

We ask that you please do not bring gifts to the party. In lieu of gifts, your artistic talents and financial patronage will support important humanitarian causes.

Admittance to the event for any artists donating their personal work is, naturally, free.

Those wishing to attend in a non-artistic capacity (i.e., you forget to make or bring your own art) will be asked to make a suggested donation of $20. We strongly prefer that all invitees challenge themselves to produce some piece of art and donate it to our auction, no matter their skill level.

Please only bring art that is your own work, and which you are prepared to give away. Please only bring art that you are also prepared to take home with you. Artistic collaboration with other attendees of the event is perfectly acceptable.

Types of Art Our Gallery Collects

As this is a gallery, not a museum, the items contributed to our collection must have some sort of physical embodiment. Digital artworks such as illustrations or photography should be printed out, though it is acceptable to send a copy of the digital originals to the purchaser in addition to the prints.

Performed works such as songs or interpretive dances may be acceptable, so long as some component (e.g., lyrics) can be purchased and taken home.

We only accept finished artworks, so please do not plan on making additions to your project during the event. We do not have a color printer or high quality paper at the gallery.

Attending the Event

Please arrive around 6pm with your artwork in hand. If you are not sure of the gallery’s address, please contact one of the staff members (Max or Travis).

You are welcome to invite your partners or friends to this event. They are also strongly encouraged to produce and submit an art piece for the auction. Please direct them to this website and ensure that they fill out the RSVP form ↗.

The gallery requires patrons to be up to date ↗ on all vaccinations, including (but not limited to) a vaccine for SARS-CoV-2, the virus that causes COVID-19.
